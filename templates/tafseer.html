Tafseer & Translation

Welcome to the Tafseer & Translation course! This course is designed for students who wish to deepen their understanding of the Qur'an through its interpretation and translation. Our experienced instructors will guide you through the meanings and context of the verses.

Course Details:

  • Duration: 3 months
  • Classes: 2 times a week
  • Location: Physical/Online

Course Structure:

The course is divided into several modules, each focusing on different aspects of Tafseer and translation:

  • Module 1: Introduction to Tafseer
  • Module 2: Historical Context of the Qur'an
  • Module 3: Key Themes and Messages
  • Module 4: Translation Techniques
  • Module 5: Practical Application and Discussion

Each module includes video lessons, quizzes, and practice sessions to ensure you grasp the concepts effectively.
