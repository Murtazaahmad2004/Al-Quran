Our Tajweed course is designed to help you master the rules of Qur'anic recitation. Here are the key features:

  • Learn the rules of Tajweed in a structured manner.
  • Practice with certified teachers who provide personalized feedback.
  • Interactive lessons with audio and video resources.
  • Flexible scheduling to fit your lifestyle.

Join us to enhance your recitation skills and deepen your connection with the Qur'an!

Course Structure

The course is divided into several modules, each focusing on different aspects of Tajweed:

  • Module 1: Introduction to Tajweed
  • Module 2: Rules of Noon and Meem
  • Module 3: Rules of Qalqalah
  • Module 4: Advanced Tajweed Techniques
  • Module 5: Practical Application and Recitation Practice

Each module includes video lessons, quizzes, and practice sessions to ensure you grasp the concepts effectively.
