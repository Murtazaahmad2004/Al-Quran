About Us

Welcome to Al-Quran Academy, your trusted platform for learning and understanding the Holy Qur’an. Our mission is to make Qur’an education accessible to everyone — children, adults, and new Muslims — through structured online courses, certified teachers, and interactive lessons. We offer a range of programs including: Qur’an Reading (Nazra) Tajweed and Pronunciation Hifz (Memorization) Qur’anic Tafsir and Translation Courses for Kids and Adults Whether you're a beginner or looking to deepen your connection with the Qur’an, our goal is to guide you with authentic knowledge, modern tools, and spiritual insight — all from the comfort of your home.
