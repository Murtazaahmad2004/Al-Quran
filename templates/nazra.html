Welcome to the Nazra-ul-Quran Course

This course is designed to help you read and understand the Quran in its original Arabic script.

We will cover the following topics:

  • 1. Introduction to Arabic letters and sounds
  • 2. Basic Tajweed rules
  • 3. Reading practice with Quranic verses
  • 4. Understanding the meaning of selected verses

Course Details:

Duration: 6 months

Classes: 2 times a week

Time: 5:00 PM - 6:30 PM

Location: Physical/Online

Instructor: Sheikh Naveed
