🌟 Learn More About Our Courses

📚 1. Course Details

We offer a wide range of Qur’an courses tailored for all levels:

  • Tajweed Course – Learn correct pronunciation and articulation.
  • Hifz-ul-Qur’an – Memorize the Qur’an step by step with guidance.
  • Tafsir & Translation – Understand the meaning and context of verses.
  • Nazra (Basic Reading) – Perfect for beginners learning to read.
  • Qira’at – Explore different styles of Qur’anic recitation.

Levels Available: Beginner | Intermediate | Advanced
Course Duration: Flexible — from 1 month to full-year programs.
