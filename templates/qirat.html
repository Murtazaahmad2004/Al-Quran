Welcome to the Qira’at Course

This course is designed to help you read and understand the Quran in its original Arabic script.

We will cover the following topics:

  • 1. Intro to Qira’at – Understanding the concept, differences, and history.
  • 2. Learning 1 or more Qira’at – Usually starts with Ḥafs ʿan ʿĀṣim.
  • 3. Advanced Sanad Courses – For those seeking certification in reciting Qira’at with a chain going back to the Prophet ﷺ.
  • 4. Understanding the meaning of selected verses

Course Details:

Duration: 8 months

Classes: 4 times a week

Time: 4:00 PM - 5:30 PM

Location: Physical/Online

Instructor: Sheikh Nadeem
